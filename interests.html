I hope to further devleop these skills in the future, and eventually apply them in professional settings.

Privacy & Anonymity

Soverignty Over One's Data

For the longest time, I've had a bit of paranoia around what kinds of data my interaction with different internet services leaves behind, and how this is not only my property but also my responsibility to protect. Because of this, I've become highly interested in different anonymity and virtualization technologies, and how these work on the inside. Tor, I2P, and amnestic operating systems are some examples.
